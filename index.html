Heavy showers in UP

The state of Uttar Pradesh has been receiving moderate to heavy showers from the past two days. Mainly northern districts including Bareilly, Moradabad, have been receiving good rains. However, southern districts have also received scattered light showers.

Wind in Delhi/NCR Region

The axis of Monsoon trough has now shifted towards the foothills of Himalayas. Thus, now, westerly winds are blowing over Delhi and the NCR region. These westerly winds are relatively drier in nature, humidity levels have also decreased.

Good rain in Odisha

Odisha has been receiving good rainfall since last many days. At present rainfall of Odisha is normal. During the second half of July, a Low-Pressure area developed over Odisha coast which gave moderate to heavy showers leading flooding in many districts.

Moderate showers

Coastal areas of Andhra Pradesh have received moderate showers with one or two heavy spells since last two days. But the weather conditions of interior parts of the state remained almost dry.
